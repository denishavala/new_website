How We Work

Our Streamlined Manufacturing Process

From the initial engineering query to dispatch, we maintain a highly structured process to ensure perfect components and seamless global shipping.

01
1. Research

We analyze your CAD drawing, specifications, tolerances, and volume requirements.

02
2. Planning

Our tooling engineers plan the production cycle and configure the CNC coordinates.

03
3. Design

Initial samples are machined, tested, and shipped to you for dimensional signoff.

04
4. Production

High-speed automatic lathes and multi-axis CNC machines begin mass production.

05
5. Testing

Every batch is checked under digital profile projectors and threads go/no-go gauges.

06
6. Launch

Parts are packed in anti-tarnish bags, boxed, and exported via sea/air freight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find prompt answers regarding our manufacturing capacity, quality certifications, shipping routes, and custom options.

We work with high-quality brass grades including IS 319 (Type 1), Free Cutting Brass (CZ121 / CW614N), Lead-Free Brass (CW511L), CDA 360, and custom alloys matching European RoHS and REACH environmental specifications.
Yes! We accept CAD drawings in multiple formats including STEP (.stp), IGES (.igs), SolidWorks (.sldprt), AutoCAD (.dwg), and PDF. Our tooling engineers will verify the dimensions and compile machine coordinates.
Our MOQ typically ranges from 1,000 units for mid-sized turned items to 10,000 units for smaller brass inserts or terminal pins. However, we do accept lower quantities for initial prototype validation runs.
Our facility maintains ISO 9001:2015 standards. We perform statistical quality control (SQC) at every stage. We use micro-calipers, ring/plug gauges, and digital profile projectors. A complete Inspection Report and Material Test Certificate (MTC) are attached to every commercial export dispatch.
We support EXW, FOB, CIF, and DDU terms. Smaller batches are shipped via Air Express (DHL/FedEx), while container dispatches go via Jamnagar/Mundra ports to destinations worldwide including Europe, the USA, and Asia.
